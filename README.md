# **Spotify--Data-Analysis-Excel-Power-BI-SQL-**

# **Introduction**
This project involve Data Cleaning and Transformation in Excel ,Extraction and validation in SQL , Spotify Dashboard creation in Power BI to analyze and visualize music streaming data. It provides insights into the most streamed tracks, top-performing artists, yearly trends, and more. The dashboard is designed for stakeholders to make data-driven decisions about music performance and trends.

# **Overview**
- Title: SPOTIFY ANALYSIS
- Key metrics displayed at the top:
    - Average bpm (beats per minute): 122.54
    - Average danceability percentage: 66.97
    - Total sum of streams: 490 billion

# **Conclusion**
The Spotify Analysis dashboard effectively summarizes streaming performance and audio attributes of music tracks, highlighting key insights:

- Popular artists: The Weeknd and Taylor Swift dominate streaming volumes.
- Danceability impact: Tracks with higher danceability percentage tend to generate more streams.
- Energy distribution: The top tracks exhibit varied energy levels, with “Numb” leading in energy percentage.
- Release trends: Track releases peak in certain months, influencing overall stream counts.
- Overall metrics: An average bpm of 122.54 and danceability of 66.97 characterize the catalog, with a massive 490 billion total streams.

The analysis can guide music curation, marketing strategies, or playlist optimization by focusing on high‑energy, danceable tracks from popular artists.
